## 5. MODELS TRAINED

Script: /Source Codes/train_models.py
Training Date: 2026-06-28 17:07:22
Device Used: GPU
Training Time: 2.7 mins

Architecture:
  Single dual-head mBERT model (bert-base-multilingual-cased)
  - Shared BERT encoder (lower 6 layers frozen)
  - Head 1: Linear(768, 3) — Sentiment classification
  - Head 2: Linear(768, 5) — Aspect classification
  - Combined loss = sentiment_loss + aspect_loss

  Name:         Dual-Head mBERT (Sentiment)
